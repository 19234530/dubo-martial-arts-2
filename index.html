About Our DuBo

Founded in 2005, DuBo Martial Arts has been helping students of all ages develop discipline, confidence, and physical fitness through traditional and modern martial arts training.

Our 5,000 sq ft facility features state-of-the-art training equipment, multiple training areas, and a welcoming environment for beginners and advanced practitioners alike.
